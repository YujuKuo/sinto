關於新東

About TaiwanSintong

台灣新東機械股份有限公司，係經由日本新東工業株式會社引進先進工業技術所成立之專業設備及投射材製造商，我們致力於生產離岸風電專用噴砂設備、環保設備、投射材。

我們擁有最先進的生產設備與環境，在每一生產過程中，皆經嚴密的品質管控，符合國際規範，並以堅強的國際背景技衛陣容，提供專業的服務，為您創造價值同時為綠能科技盡一份心力。

AIWAN SINTONG MACHINERY CO., LTD. is established by JAPAN SINTOKOGIO, LTD with an advanced technology, which provide the professional equipment and high-quality abrasive media. We are devoted in manufacturing casting equipment, blasting equipment, recycling equipment and abrasive media for Offshore wind power industry.

We are over 4 decades of manufacturing experience in producing steel shots and steel grits. We can provide you a solution and expertise in variety of industries which include casting industry, iron/steel industry, transportation, energy, granite cutting, locomotive manufacture, stone industries, and more.

TAIWAN SINTONG MACHINERY CO., LTD always keep in mind to give back to the environment by not only manufacturing and installation of machineries, but also by making sure that we do not cause any negative impact on our environment and making sure we have a sustainable eco-system.
